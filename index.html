Our Mission

The KOREE program aims to empower young girls with tools and strategies for life, using their menstrual cycle as a framework for understanding their cyclical nature.

🌸 Inform & Empower

Inform young women about the biology of the menstrual cycle and provide practical skills for managing menstrual health while dispelling myths and misinformation.

🌙 Track & Plan

Connect your cycle to lunar phases without requiring personal data input. Understand your body's natural rhythm through universal moon tracking.

✨ Educate for the Future

Educate about puberty, fertility, and reproductive health, empowering informed decisions and reducing risks through knowledge.

The Four Phases of Your Cycle

Each phase offers unique strengths and insights. Learn to listen to your body and flow with its natural energy, synchronized with the moon's rhythm.

🌑

Menstrual

New Moon Phase

Time for rest, reflection, and intuition. Your body releases what is no longer needed. Connect with inner wisdom and set intentions.

Key Practice:

Supta Baddha Konasana

🌒

Post-Menstrual

Waxing Crescent

Energy and curiosity rise. Time to produce, create, and look outward. Perfect for planning and trying new things with renewed clarity.

Key Practice:

Seated Twist (Boundaries)

🌕

Ovulatory

Full Moon Phase

Peak energy and confidence. Stronger desire to socialize and connect. Most generous phase - perfect for collaboration and sharing.

Key Practice:

Bridge Pose (Confidence)

🌖

Pre-Menstrual

Waning Moon

Emotionally intense time that reveals what truly matters. Increased sensitivity highlights core values - a powerful guide if listened to constructively.

Key Practice:

Creative Expression & Dance
